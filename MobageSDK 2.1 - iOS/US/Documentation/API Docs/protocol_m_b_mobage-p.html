Initialize the Mobage service, and report a Mobage analytics event indicating that the app has started.

Call this method immediately after your app starts.

Important: Call this method only once.

Parameters:
serverEnvironmentIndicates whether to connect to Mobage's sandbox or production environment. Important: Before you submit your app to Mobage for review, you must change this value to refer to Mobage's production environment.
appIdThe app ID, also known as the "app key." Generated by the Mobage Developer Portal.
appVersionThe app version.
consumerKeyThe app's OAuth key for accessing the Mobage service. Generated by the Mobage Developer Portal.
consumerSecretThe app's OAuth secret for accessing the Mobage service. Generated by the Mobage Developer Portal.
