Welcome!

Welcome to the Mobage Native SDK for iOS! The Native SDK connects your app to Mobage, the premier social gaming platform for smartphones. Mobage connects your users to a huge community of fun-seekers that circles the globe. With the Native SDK, you can access a Mobage user's social graph; sell virtual items to users; and harness Mobage's powerful analytics service.
